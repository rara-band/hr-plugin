### Why HR matters

A company is, in the end, its people. Get the team wrong — too many, too few, or people on the wrong things — and it struggles no matter how good each person is. That's why every company has an **HR team**: not a seat that does the work, but one that watches whether *the team is shaped to fit the work.*

Left alone, a team quietly drifts:

- People **only get added, rarely let go.** A seat that mattered once just stays.
- **What you're trying to do changes, but the team stays the same.** The people you need and the people you have drift apart.
- The more people there are, **the more time goes just into keeping everyone in sync.** Pile on more and it gets slower, not faster.

And here's the catch — **nobody on the team is minding this.** Everyone's busy with their own work; no one stops to ask "do we still need this seat? is this the right team for where we're headed?" And there's no fixed formula for the answer — whether to cut, merge, or hire depends on the situation.

> So **someone has to watch the team as a whole, one step back.** That's HR, and that's why it matters — how a team is shaped decides whether the work actually gets done.

**Agents are no different.** Put a bunch of agents together to work as a team, and that team drifts the same way. Agent HR is the HR for your agent team.

### So, what it does

It looks over your agent team — who's idle, who's swamped, what's missing, which seats no longer fit where you're going — and **tells you what to change.** But *you* decide; it never touches anything on its own.

Every suggestion comes with **why it sees it that way (which records it looked at) and what you can expect** if you act on it.

### What it promises

- **Suggests only.** It never changes things itself — you decide.
- **Speaks only from what it can actually see.** What it couldn't see, it says so.
- **Doesn't grade its own work.**
- **Reads the situation, not a formula.**
- **Works the same on Claude and Codex.**

### Try it

```
/plugin marketplace add rara-band/hr-plugin
/plugin install hr-plugin@rara-band
/hr-review
```

`/hr-review` gathers your team's records, looks them over, and hands back a grounded HR note.
